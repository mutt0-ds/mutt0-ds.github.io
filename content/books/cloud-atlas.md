From https://www.nateliason.com/notes/cloud-atlas-david-mitchell, excellent book. The film didn't convince me, the book is well written, though a couple of parts were heavy (future and caveman). Nice idea of linking the various stories, it is much clearer in the book, but I don't see a huge impact from the previous chapters (apart from Somni becoming a deity). Good book overall, enjoyable. In my view the film was not really necessary, difficult material to handle.
