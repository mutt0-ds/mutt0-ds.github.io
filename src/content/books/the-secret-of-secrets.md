A nice, readable book with a twist I did not expect and the usual hypnotic Langdon story with a lost mystery. Brown writes well and keeps you engaged. It is not his best story; I preferred Inferno, but better than Origins. Too many explanations on noetics when [**SPOILER**], so I skipped those sections. A pleasant read, with a few rough edges and cliches, but overall very good. I appreciated the inclusion of real people, like the editor, a nice touch.
